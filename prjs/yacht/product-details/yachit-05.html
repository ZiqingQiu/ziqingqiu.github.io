SUNSEEKER 30M YACHT — "SIMPLE PLEASURE" Back To Inventory

This unique 30M Yacht with brown hull and white superstructure is built to MCA charter regulations and is extensively specified. No expense has been spared by its meticulous Owner, as a result "SIMPLE PLEASURE" is in stunning condition. The RINA five years survey has just been successfully completed. This vessel provides luxury, style and comfort in abundance and will perfectly suit your program whether you are planning extensive cruises or days at anchor. The vast saloon can easily accommodate all the Guests, the four cabins are all en-suite. There is a good sized crew accommodation for up to five crew members which a very important factor when it comes to high end service during charters. For fun outdoors choose the sundeck and its jacuzzi or the hydraulic bathing platform and the garage accommodating your water toys. Performance is as impressive as the interior is luxurious. Viewings are highly recommended, “SIMPLE PLEASURE” won’t fail to impress.

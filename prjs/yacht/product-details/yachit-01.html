BENETTI 46 METRE — "MISTRESS" Back To Inventory

This imposing, custom built 46m Benetti Yacht, represents remarkable value when compared to vessels of the same size and condition. “MISTRESS” has recently concluded her refit and boasts an interior designed by Zuretti, who utilised many pieces from the world renowned Versace range, this has been complemented with exterior styling by Stefano Natucci. This has combined to create a beautifully crafted and truly timeless boat, which puts a particular emphasis on relaxation and entertainment. The formal seating in the lower saloon, offers sumptuous and well appointed furnishings which compliment the style of this unique boat. This elegance is continued to the upper saloon which boasts a relaxed seating area together with a large bar and games table. The outside areas are of particular note with ample space for dining on each of the three decks. With a large upper deck featuring wraparound seating this is a fantastic entertaining space, enhanced by a large jaccuzzi and ample bar with matching parasols. The sheer volume below deck allows her to include a gym and sauna with ample space for all her water toys.
